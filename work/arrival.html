Arrival

In collaboration with the Arrival team, we crafted a new identity and interface design for the Arrival app.

What we worked on

UI Design Brand Identity App Design Brand Strategy

Year completed

2023

Background

Arrival introduces users to a world of seamless financial management, offering comprehensive services right at their fingertips. Positioned as the future of finance, Arrival's revolutionary platform provides users with unparalleled convenience and accessibility. It marks the beginning of a transformative financial journey, empowering users to take control of their finances like never before.

Challenges

The design team faced the complex task of harmonizing diverse financial services into a cohesive platform while navigating intricate regulatory landscapes. They grappled with the delicate balance of incorporating advanced functionality while maintaining a user-friendly design. Challenges also arose in ensuring compliance with financial regulations across various regions, adding an extra layer of complexity to the project.

Outcome

Despite these hurdles, Arrival emerged as a groundbreaking financial app that surpassed expectations. Its intuitive interface and comprehensive suite of services offer unparalleled convenience and accessibility. Arrival's seamless money transfers, real-time currency exchange, and robust budgeting tools empower users to manage their finances effortlessly. Furthermore, Arrival's unwavering commitment to security and compliance ensures users' peace of mind. Overall, Arrival redefines financial management apps, providing a transformative experience that empowers users on their financial journey.
